Privacy Policy for Sodbridge Ltd

At Sodbridge, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, academy, and services.

Information We Collect

  • We collect personal details, such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information, only when you voluntarily provide them.
  • We collect information on how you use our website and services, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction times, to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • We use your information to provide and personalize our services, process transactions, communicate updates, and enhance your experience with us.
  • Your information helps us improve our courses, respond to inquiries, and maintain our business operations effectively.

Data Protection and Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • We never sell your information and only share it with trusted third-party providers when necessary for operational purposes.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us if you wish to exercise these rights or if you have concerns about your data.

Updates to This Policy

  • We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
